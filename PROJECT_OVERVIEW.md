# 🏦 Intelli-Credit — Project Overview

## Introduction

**Intelli-Credit** is an AI-powered credit decisioning platform designed to automate the preparation of a **Credit Appraisal Memorandum (CAM)** for MSME and corporate loan applications.

The system addresses a major challenge in the corporate lending ecosystem: the **Data Paradox**, where large volumes of financial data exist but require significant manual effort to analyze.

Traditional credit appraisal processes often take **days or weeks** and rely heavily on manual document review. Intelli-Credit introduces an **AI-driven pipeline** that automates data extraction, financial analysis, risk scoring, and CAM generation while maintaining **human oversight through Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) decision checkpoints**.

---

## Problem Statement

In the Indian corporate lending ecosystem, credit officers must analyze a wide range of information before approving a loan:

### Structured Data
- GST filings
- Income Tax Returns (ITRs)
- Bank statements

### Unstructured Data
- Annual reports
- Financial statements
- Board meeting minutes
- Rating agency reports
- Shareholding patterns

### External Intelligence
- Sector news and macroeconomic signals
- MCA (Ministry of Corporate Affairs) filings
- Litigation records from the e-Courts portal

### Primary Insights
- Site visit observations
- Management interviews
- Operational assessments

Processing and synthesizing this information manually is **time-consuming and error-prone**, often causing delays and increasing the risk of overlooking critical signals.

---

## Solution

The **Intelli-Credit Engine** automates this workflow using a layered AI architecture.

Key capabilities include:

- Automated **document ingestion and classification**
- Intelligent **financial data extraction**
- Cross-verification of financial records
- AI-based **risk scoring and credit analysis**
- Explainable AI outputs for transparency
- Automated **Credit Appraisal Memo (CAM)** generation
- Governance monitoring and model oversight

The system ensures that AI supports human decision-making rather than replacing it.

---

## Core Features

### Document Intelligence
Extracts and parses financial data from:

- PDFs
- scanned images
- CSV/Excel files
- structured financial records

---

### Financial Analysis Engine

The platform automatically derives financial metrics including:

- revenue growth
- profitability ratios
- debt ratios
- liquidity indicators
- cash flow patterns

---

### AI Risk Scoring

The risk engine evaluates borrower profiles using:

- financial indicators
- external research signals
- qualitative analysis
- explainable ML scoring models

The model provides **transparent explanations for each recommendation**.

---

### Web Research Agent

The system performs **secondary research** using web search APIs to identify:

- adverse news
- sector risks
- regulatory changes
- litigation history

This ensures credit analysis incorporates **real-world contextual intelligence**.

---

### Human-in-the-Loop (HITL)

Critical checkpoints allow credit officers to:

- verify extracted data
- adjust financial ratios
- review AI recommendations
- override automated decisions

This guarantees **human accountability in final credit decisions**.

---

### Automated CAM Generation

Once analysis is complete, the system generates a **Credit Appraisal Memorandum (CAM)** that includes:

- borrower profile
- financial analysis
- risk assessment
- AI insights
- final recommendation

The report can be exported as:

- PDF
- DOCX
- JSON

---

### Governance & Monitoring

The system includes a governance dashboard that tracks:

- model performance
- portfolio risk distribution
- drift indicators
- decision audit logs

This layer ensures the platform remains **compliant with responsible AI practices**.

---

## Impact

The Intelli-Credit platform reduces credit analysis time from **weeks to minutes** while improving:

- transparency
- accuracy
- scalability
- governance

By combining **AI intelligence with human oversight**, Intelli-Credit represents a next-generation approach to corporate credit appraisal.
